About the Skin Cancer Detection System

The Skin Cancer Detection System is a powerful tool designed to assist in early detection and diagnosis of skin cancer. It utilizes advanced algorithms and image processing techniques to analyze skin images and identify potential cancerous lesions.

Our system aims to improve the accuracy and efficiency of skin cancer detection, enabling early intervention and improved patient outcomes.

Key Features

  • Accurate detection of potential skin cancer lesions based on image analysis.
  • User-friendly interface for easy uploading and processing of skin images.
